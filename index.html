About Monkey Sushi

 

The talented staff at Monkey Sushi is committed to providing each customer with high-quality Japanese cuisine and exceptional service. We understand that quality ingredients are the foundation for great sushi, which is why we will never compromise on the quality and standards of the ingredients we use. We source our food from local farmers and work tirelessly to ensure that the care that goes into producing the food shines through in our cuisine.

 

With over 100 different dishes offered, you can be sure to find something delicious each time you visit. Stop in today for a great dining experience, or call us to request a take-out order.
